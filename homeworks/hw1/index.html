Reading Response, Anne Zhang

1. What is something you learned from this article?

One thing I learned from this article was that web/app design considers the different experiences each user goes through. For example, AirBnB having London in the search bar could be a visual indicator for people who aren't as familiar with the app to know what this bar is for. It's a good reminder that not everyone processes information the same way.

2. What was your favorite part of this article?

My favorite part was when the author talked about copying designs when she had no idea why they worked. It helps emphasize her point of how we should analyze apps we engage with usually. Also, as a graphic designer, I definitely understand the impulse of wanting to create something visually stunning over what might be practical.
